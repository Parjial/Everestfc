Where do we Participate

Golden League

Delhi

The Golden League is official grassroots football league, launched by ‘Football Delhi’. The purpose of the league is to broad base participation and provide a structure to football at grassroots.

Khelo India Football Delhi Grassroot League

Delhi

Khelo India Football Delhi Grassroot League is providing platform for young talents to showcase their talent. It is a great initiative to develop and grow grassroot level football in India. As we can see it is providing great exposure to players participating.

Delhi League

Delhi

The Delhi Football League is a ladder-based state football competition organised by 'Football Delhi', involving a total of four divisions and over 80 teams in the Indian state of Delhi. After a certain age we provide base for children to participate in such league. It is a great stage for a football player to build and grow his/her roots.

RSA Cup

Delhi

Absolute focus on football development assist in identification, grooming and development of talented players. Promote sports culture among the youth, starting as early as 6 years of age through football.

State Championship

Haryana

Players from our club are easily selected for their district teams to play state championship. State championship are very helpful for players to improve in their game. It also provides support for other aspects of their career mostly in education.

YMCA Sports Cup

Faridabad

This Tournament is organised by our club every Year. Numerous club participate in this tournament to secure the first position. Many Scouts are also invited to help players get into their dream club.

Why Join Our Club

Professional coaching

We provide access to professional coaching and training, which can help players improve their skills, technique, and overall fitness levels.

Competitive experience

We help players to gain experience in a competitive environment, testing their skills against other teams and players of varying levels.

Personal development

Joining our football club will have a positive impact on personal development, as players learn important life skills such as discipline, time management, and goal setting.

Opportunities for Professional Career

We offer opportunities for players to advance their skills and careers, with pathways to higher levels of competition, professional contracts, or even international representation

Team Building

Football is a team sport, and playing for a club helps players develop their teamwork, communication, and leadership skills, as well as build relationships and friendships with their teammates.

Social Benefits

Joining a football club can provide players with a sense of belonging and community, as they connect with other like-minded individuals who share their passion for the sport.
